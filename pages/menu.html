TuneGuessr Info

Tuneguessr is a simple game where you are given a song from a randomly picked country.
Select which country you thing the song is from (or is about in certain cases). Check your guess either with the button in the sidebar or by smacking the spacebar like Rainbolt (be sure to say "Okay, we'll take that" after each smack). Tuneguessr saves and displays statistics about your guesses, as well as a username that you choose. Stats and usernames are ONLY saved locally in this browser. This game uses Google Analytics to gain insight on player count.
